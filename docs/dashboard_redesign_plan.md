# Power BI Dashboard Redesign Plan

## Page 1: Executive Overview

- KPI cards: Net Revenue, Product Revenue, Orders, Customers, AOV, Repeat Customer Rate.
- Line chart: Monthly Net Revenue.
- Bar chart: Top Countries by Net Revenue.
- Callout: November seasonality and UK revenue concentration.

## Page 2: Customer Segmentation

- RFM segment distribution.
- Segment table: Customers, Revenue, AOV, Recency, Frequency.
- Recommended actions by segment:
  - Champions: loyalty/VIP offers.
  - At Risk: win-back campaigns.
  - New Customers: second-purchase incentive.

## Page 3: Market and Product Performance

- Country revenue vs AOV.
- Top products by Product Revenue, excluding postage.
- Postage revenue as a separate KPI.
- Callout: avoid treating postage as a top product.

## Page 4: Retention and Revenue Leakage

- Monthly cohort retention heatmap.
- Return/cancellation rows by country.
- Gross vs net revenue explanation.
- Callout: monitor cancellations and returns as leakage KPIs.

## Visual Design Notes

- Keep the dashboard business-facing, not decorative.
- Use explicit titles that state the question answered by each visual.
- Avoid crowded pie charts for country/product comparisons.
- Add short text annotations near each key finding.
